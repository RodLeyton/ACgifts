Where can I find the logfile?

Logs are located in the application directorys 'data' subfolder.
When the primary log reaches 10kB it is moved to 'log-release.bak.txt' and a new file is started.
The exact location will depend on your OS, but the easiest way to find it is to click the Dir or Log buttons on the Edit form.
