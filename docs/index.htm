We have a new toy for AC fans!

Wether you trade gifts just on your main or have many fuel rings to maintain, tracking gifts can become difficult and time consuming.
Speadsheets can do the job sure, but quickly get out of hand and bloated especially as your game count increases.

Introducing ACgifts

A simple app that keeps count of gifts sent and recieved, sorted by groups (such as fuel rings)

Neighbors can be imported via CSV file (template supplied) or directly entered and edited in app.

Free and open source

The project is hosted on GitHub feel free to assist in the development. Although I will not be spending much more time adding features.

Quick and easy setup

Packaging and deployment using Microsoft ClickOnce technology enables simple install without requiring elevated priviledges (non-admin) along with automatic and rapid updates.

Microsoft .NET6 is required, but you should be directed during install if your PC does not have it already. (otherwise find it here)
