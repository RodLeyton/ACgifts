We have a new toy for Airport City fans!

Whether you trade gifts just on your main, or have many fuel rings to maintain, tracking neighbors and gifts eventually becomes difficult and time consuming.
Of course speadsheets can do the job, but you too will likely find they get out of hand and bloated, especially as your game count increases.
And naturally the larger the spreadsheet gets, the longer it takes.

Maybe you don't care what you recieve, but just want to keep track of who you should send to knowing that AC favourites cannot be trusted to do the job.

Does your spreadsheet keep count of gifts, does it remember the last time one was sent, how about recieved?
Will it warn you if you drop off your neighbors Fav's list? We know that can happen quite unexpectantly for no reason.
How about if your neighbor dissapears off your fav list through no fault of your own? Nobody wants to be that guy...

Or have you just given up and relinquished to the 'fact' that it is just too much to do, and "close enough is good enough"?

Maybe you (like me) wrote macros for buttons, but it still had so many columns it took up almost all the screen, and in a moment of unclarity accidentally clicked the wrong one...
Can your spreadsheet undo it? Neighter could mine... but Acgifts can!

Introducing ACgifts

A simple and very lightweight windows app (~700kb) app that keeps count of gifts sent and recieved, sorted by groups (such as fuel rings)
Neighbors can be imported via CSV file or directly entered and edited in app.
And it is easier, faster and much much smaller on the screen (try resizing to minimum width - it still has all you need!)

Free and open source

The project is written in C# and hosted on GitHub and contributors are very wellcome. Contact me via the forum or Discord to request write access to the repo.
The best feature of open source code is you can view it, compile it and modify it to your liking, and I encourage you to do so!
You don't even have to ask to do so, just download and go for it! (But I hope you'd contribute and help to improve it)
This repo compiles in Visual Studio 2022 community (free) without a single warning or code quality reccomendation.

Quick and easy setup

Packaging and deployment using Microsoft ClickOnce enables simple install without requiring elevated priviledges (non-admin) along with automatic and rapid updates.
Microsoft .NET6 is required, you will be directed during install if your PC does not have it already. (if not find it here)
Be forewarned you will recieve 2 warnings during install (Unrecognised app and Unknown Publisher). Free software can be expensive to write if you pay for a EV code signing certificate.

Change log

See the change log for a description of bugfixes and features added to the latest release, as well as upcoming features.

 

Send/Recv (main) window

  • Find your neighbors easily with filtering by group and 7 sorting methods.
  • Find a specific neighbor and associated accounts across all groups with only 2 clicks.
  • Right click to undo a mistaken send or recv.
  • You can send to all with a single click, and 'all but' a specific neighbor via the context menu (right click).
  • Keep track of how many gifts this session, by group and in total.
  • Column visibility, order and widths are saved, as is the form size and position so once you get it just right it'll always be just right for you :)
  • Neighbors that have been less reliable are highlighted in red.

 

Builtin editor allows easy addition, deletion or modification of data

  • Backup/Restore your data quickly, as well as import/export to csv.
  • Built-in logviewer, with link to your data directory.
  • Move or delete multiple rows at once making it easy to reorder groups.
  • All data is checked and validated as soon as it is entered, reducing mistakes and headaches.
  • Insert new rows anywhere in the list with right click.

Select multiple rows with the ctrl or shift keys, move up/down or delete via the right click context menu to change the list order, or even insert a new row mid list.
